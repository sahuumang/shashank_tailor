About the Shop

A name Sagar trusts for fine gents' tailoring and quality fabric.

Shashankh Tailor and Fabric brings together two things every well-dressed man needs — good cloth and a good stitch. From selecting the right fabric to taking careful measurements, every step is done with attention so the final garment fits you just right.

Whether it's a sharp suit for an occasion, a traditional sherwani or kurta for a wedding, or everyday shirts and trousers, the work is finished neatly, on time, and at a fair price.
